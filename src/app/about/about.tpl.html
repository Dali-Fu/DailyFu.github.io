One year ago as luck will have it, I stumbled upon learning about coding bootcamps. I have always had an interest in technology and programming, so in the midst of self-discovery with nothing to lose, I packed my bags, moved from one big city to another (NY - SF), and enrolled at Hack Reactor.

Subsequent to graduating the program, Hack Reactor hired me to stay on for a few months assisting the new incoming students. It has been an invigorating experience to see where I was, how far I’ve come, to eventually helping others achieve the same. I love being a part of that community but now I’m ready for my next adventure.

I am a full-stack software engineer with a focus in javascript. I have experience with frameworks such as backbone, angular, and react. I also have backend experience with node.js/express. If you have a job opportunity/lead to my next expedition, let’s grab coffee or boba!
